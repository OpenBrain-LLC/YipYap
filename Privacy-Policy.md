# **YipYap – Privacy Policy**

**Effective Date:** Mar/25/2026

## **1. Overview**

This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

## **2. Information We Collect**

### **Information you provide**

* Username and account details
* Content you post
* Messages sent through the platform

### **Information collected automatically**

* IP address
* Device and browser type
* Activity on the platform (such as interactions and usage patterns)

## **3. How We Use Information**

We use your information to:

* Operate and maintain the platform
* Improve features and performance
* Keep the platform safe and secure
* Prevent abuse and enforce our rules

## **4. Sharing of Information**

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share information:

* With service providers who help run the platform
* When required by law
* To protect users, rights, and safety

## **5. Data Security**

We use reasonable safeguards, including encryption where appropriate, to protect your data. No system is completely secure, but we take security seriously.

## **6. Data Retention**

We keep your information only as long as necessary to operate the Service and meet legal requirements.

## **7. Your Rights**

Depending on your location, you may be able to:
* Access your data
* Request corrections
* Request deletion

## **8. Children’s Privacy**

YipYap is not intended for children under 13. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it.

## **9. Changes to This Policy**

We may update this Privacy Policy. If changes are significant, we will notify users.

# **Community Guidelines**

To keep YipYap safe and enjoyable:

* Be respectful
* Don’t share private information
* Don’t harass or threaten others
* No spam or scams
* Follow the law

Breaking these rules may result in account restrictions or removal.
